About Me

I have been programming since I was in highschool. First, by taking C++ classes and later reverse engineering HTML from webpages and writing scripts in my TI-98 graphing calculator.

I went to college for film theory and for a few years worked in post production, working for independent movies, commercial production companies, a music video production company, and a tech journalism publication.

Programming and photography were both active hobbies I had and skills I developed on the side while pursuing a film post production career.

In 2008, a family member was staring up her own food truck concept and I was brought on to help with photography, web, and brand design. With help from a friend we christened the company Kogi BBQ, developed the Wordpress app, the Twitter presence, and food photo catalog. This food truck company would later start a revolution and have many others follow in what has been dubbed the "food truck craze".

Honing my skills in web development skills and photography while working for Kogi, I started freelancing and pursuing programming and photography professionally.
